Lettuce (sounds like Letters) is a font management tool, designed for designer who always feel hard to pick the right font because they have to open the design software to test the fonts there. Here I made a quick trailer for Lettuce.

Click the underline text to see how them work. After you done, plase click home button to back this page, so other users can also see it.

With this app, you can easily to ① compare font with 3 modes including overlay fonts, ② do some typography test in font playground and even ③ print it out for sharing. And of course, The necessary function for font management like ④ Customized Tags, Detect Style and Language and Preview Text are also here. Click here to see video demo.
